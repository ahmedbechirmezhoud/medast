Professionals typically have trouble detecting heart illness owing to a high degree of ambiguity and a risk factor. This model predicts an accurate diagnosis of Ischemic Heart Diseases. The system consists of 40 machine model, each trained on a subset of the training data, and stacked with a logistic regression model.
